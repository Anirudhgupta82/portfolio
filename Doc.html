Document Verification

Document verification is a process to authenticate the legitimacy of documents provided by individuals or entities. It involves verifying the accuracy and authenticity of the information presented in the document. This process is crucial in various fields such as education, employment, immigration, and finance to ensure compliance with regulations and prevent fraud.
